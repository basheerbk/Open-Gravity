About

Open Gravity — jump in front of your camera and see how high you'd go on all eight planets, the Moon, and Pluto.

Face the camera, hold still for a moment, then jump. Pose tracking measures the nose spike and scales it across ten worlds — no weight or height needed.

  • Camera tracks your skeleton in real time
  • Nose-based jump height measurement
  • All ten astronauts hop together, scaled by gravity
  • Exhibit: open a display window on the TV and a camera window on the laptop
