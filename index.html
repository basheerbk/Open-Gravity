About

Open Gravity — an exhibit where kids stand on the mark, jump, and see how high they'd go on Earth, the Moon, Mars, and Pluto.

Place a camera facing the jump spot. Each kid steps on the STAND HERE mark, holds still, then jumps. Pose tracking measures the lift from their feet and scales it across four worlds — no weight or height needed.

  • Fixed jump spot with on-screen marker
  • Feet-based measurement at the mark
  • All four astronauts hop together, scaled by gravity
  • Next kid: step on the mark again
