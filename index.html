About

Open Gravity compares your weight and jump height on Earth, the Moon, Mars, and Pluto.

Jump in front of your camera — we measure your real jump height, then show how high you'd jump on other worlds. Weaker gravity means lighter weight and bigger jumps — Pluto wins every time.
